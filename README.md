# AutoGuard Tint App

Welcome to the AutoGuard Tint App! This application is designed to showcase car tinting services and provide information about your business.

## Features

- **Navigation:** Simple and intuitive navigation bar for easy access to different sections of the app.
- **Services:** Explore the various car tinting services offered by AutoGuard Tint.
- **Gallery:** View a gallery of car tinting projects with before and after images.
- **About Us:** Learn more about AutoGuard Tint and its commitment to quality service.
- **Booking:** Easily book car tinting services through the "Book Now" section.
